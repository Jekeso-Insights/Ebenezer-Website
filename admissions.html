NURSERY

Admission Eligibility Requirements:
1. Nursey students should have turned 2 or 3 years in the enrollment year.
2. The students should speak and or understand basic language instructions and greetings.
3. The students should be potty trained

Enrollment Process and Guidelines:
1. An original birth certificate
2. Registration Fee (Non-refundable)
3. Parents should call the school directly as the requirements may differ according to age and needs of the a student. (Email: enrollment@ebenezer.co.zw | Call on: +263 773 246 139)

ECD A & B - GRADE 1-7

Admission Eligibility Requirements:
1.ECD A and B students should have turned 4 and 5 years respectively in the enrollment year.
2. Grade 1 and 2 students should have turned 6 and 7 years respectively in the enrollment year.

Enrollment Process and Guidelines:
1. Only photocopy of the birth certificate is needed for administration office to keep.
(Orientation includes tour of the facilities and selected uniform retailers to be onsite for purchase of uniforms)

WAITING LIST

When enquiring for a place and the class is full, kindly register on the waiting list by sending an email to admin@ebenezer.com and once there is a vacancy the administration will give you a call and schedule an assessment and upon passing the student will be offered a place.

Registration on waiting list:

  • Name of student
  • Date of Birth
  • Grade sought after
  • Parent/Guardian contacts
  • Name of current school
