ECD A-B

Admission Eligibility Requirements:
1. ECD A and B students should have turned 4 and 5, respectively by the 31st of August in the enrollment year.
2. The students should speak and or understand basic language instructions and greetings.
3. The students should be potty trained

Enrollment Process and Guidelines:
1. Registration Fee of 10USD, this is charged for the formal application of your child. This payment is then reflected on your child’s application/registration form.
2. An original birth certificate, baby clinic cards and one certified copy of each document for the administration office to keep.
3. Fill in the registration and acceptance form and leave it with the administration desk.
4. RSVP for the orientation date that will be advised by the Administration desk.
(Orientation includes tour of the facilities and selected uniform retailers to be onsite for purchase of uniforms)

GRADE 1-7

Admission Eligibility Requirements:
1. Places are first offered to ECD B pupils in our system and to our sibling parents who have their children in Grades 1 to 7.
2. Grade 1 and 2 students should have turned 6 and 7, respectively by the 31st of August in the enrollment year.
3. The place is secured by payment of the registration fee and filling in the registration and acceptance forms.
4. We do not enroll new students in Grade 5,6 or 7. These are exam classes.

Enrollment Process and Guidelines:
1. Registration Fee of 10USD, this is charged for the formal application of your child. This payment is then reflected on your child’s application/registration form.
2. An original birth certificate, baby clinic cards and one certified copy of each document for the administration office to keep.
3. Fill in the registration and acceptance form and leave it with the administration desk.
4. RSVP for the orientation date that will be advised by the Administration desk.
(Orientation includes tour of the facilities and selected uniform retailers to be onsite for purchase of uniforms)

SCHOOL FEES

Once you have filled in the relevant forms, school fees invoices will be sent via email before the beginning of the school term/year.

  • Fees (ECD A-B): 600 Units/term
  • Fees (Grade 1-7): 700 Units/term
  • Transport: 100 Units/term
